AIR FREIGHT SERVICES:

We pick up shipments from and to any destination worldwide (Door to Door), with international partners in over 210 locations worldwide for effective execution of the orders with stable transit times of within 3–7 working days as the case may be.

BULK MAIL

We run a hub and spoke mode of operation for bulk movement of shipments across the nation. This is a done on agreeable terms and conditions with our esteemed customers.

PROCUREMENT & DISTRIBUTION

We have Strategic Sourcing Services which help our customers reduce the overall cost of acquisition of specified products through the application of proven sourcing and purchasing strategies, best practices, people and technologies.

EXPRESS DELIVERIES

As a back up to our Air distribution across 12 locations across Nigeria, we also make use of our fleet of trucks, and vans to facilitate effective distribution within the nation with over 21 contact offices and service partners nationwide.

AIRPORT CLEARING:

We make delivery within 24 to 72 hours from confirmation of arrival of shipment to locations within Lagos. Destinations outside Lagos attract haulage value.

HAULAGE

We have required capacity to move cargo across the nation. From light weight, to heavy weight, highly valuable shipments, LTL and FTL services, our expertise and insurance cover offer great comfort to our customers for this service.
